Learning Games Competition

Motivation

This competition is aimed at designing and simulating intelligent robots that can be used as a "Materials Handling Cart" for Ebola Emergency Treatment Units as shown below. The teams are required to write a behavioural program that utilizes behavior coordination schemes, computer vision algorithms and learning algorithms.


Problem

Given a mockup of the yellow ramp and beige canvas walls similar to a Ebola treatment unit, an iRobot create placed at random location and orientation inside 112" radius has to
  • Determine the entry to the ramp and door (Can't use artificial landmarks)
  • Speed up to get over the doorway threshold and avoid falling off the ramp
  • Stop within 28" after crossing the ramp
  • Succeed with in 1 minute

My team has succeeded all ten trials with different orientations and positions within 30 seconds and won the first prize for this competition.
